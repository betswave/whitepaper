# Roadmap

This roadmap summarizes Betswave’s phased progression from foundation work to mainnet launch and ecosystem expansion. Each phase includes: what has been achieved, why it matters, and how WAVE’s utility layer is strengthened through platform readiness and participation.

---

## 2023 – 2024 | Foundation Phase (Completed)

### What we have achieved

- Market research and product ideation completed
- Core betting protocol and architecture designed
- MVP developed and tested on testnet
- Whitepaper, tokenomics, and governance frameworks finalized

### Why this matters

- Validates the core betting and settlement flow before launch
- Reduces technical and execution risk
- Establishes clear rules for token supply and governance

### How WAVE gains value

- Fixed supply and utility defined before launch
- Clear separation between betting (USDC) and token utility

---

## 2025 | Infrastructure & Partnerships

### What we have achieved

- Collaboration within the Hedera ecosystem
- Infrastructure improvements and performance optimization
- Security reviews and internal testing
- Early ecosystem partnerships and outreach

### Why this matters

- Prepares the platform for mainnet usage
- Ensures stability and reliability for real users
- Builds ecosystem credibility before token launch

### How WAVE gains value

- Token utility planning aligned with platform readiness
- Governance and staking preparation begins

---

## 2026 Q1 | Core Platform & Token Launch

### In progress (current milestone)

- WAVE token launch and initial distribution
- Betswave mainnet launch
- USDC-based peer-to-peer (P2P) live betting
- Sports predictions and quick-play games launched

### Why this matters

- Marks the transition from development to live usage
- Enables transparent, non-custodial betting
- Activates governance and platform utilities

### How WAVE gains value

- WAVE staking required for governance participation
- Locked tokens reduce circulating supply
- Platform usage increases demand for utility

---

## 2026 Q2 – Q3 | Ecosystem Expansion

### Planned deliverables

- Betswave Casino launched
- Esports tournaments and community events introduced
- Sports and event prediction markets added
- Creator and entertainment experiences launched

### Why this matters

- Expands platform use cases and user base
- Increases overall platform activity
- Strengthens community engagement

### How WAVE gains value

- Increased staking participation
- Usage-based rewards tied to real activity
- Governance activity grows with platform expansion

---

## 2026 Q4 | Advanced Products

### Planned deliverables

- Betswave City (Play-to-Earn experiences) introduced
- Advanced trading and futures-style features launched

### Why this matters

- Adds deeper engagement for experienced users
- Expands product offerings without changing the core model

### How WAVE gains value

- Additional platform utilities increase holding and staking demand
- Governance participation continues to grow

---

## 2027+ | Long-Term Vision

### Planned deliverables

- Exploration of metaverse-based experiences
- Exploration of real-world and offline initiatives
- Planning for extended ecosystem integrations

### Why this matters

- Opens future growth paths beyond core betting
- Keeps the long-term vision flexible and adaptive

### How WAVE gains value

- WAVE remains the core governance and utility layer
- Long-term usage and staking support sustainability
