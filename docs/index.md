# Whitepaper

Betswave is a decentralized sports betting and gaming platform built on Hedera. It is designed to provide a fair, transparent, and non-custodial betting experience.

All betting on Betswave is conducted in **stablecoins** for stable, easy-to-understand payouts. Betswave’s native token, **WAVE**, is used for **governance**, **rewards**, and **platform benefits**.

## What this whitepaper covers

This document explains:

- The Betswave vision and how the platform works
- The market opportunity and why a decentralized, user-controlled betting model matters
- The utilities and design principles behind **stablecoin betting**, **WAVE**, and **ORCA**
- Governance, fee transparency, and privacy-first access principles
- The roadmap and key definitions used across the platform
