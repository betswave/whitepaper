## Abstract

BetsWave is a decentralized data processing and analytics services platform built on the Hedera Hashgraph distributed ledger network. The platform is designed to address well-documented structural inefficiencies in traditional centralized data service markets — including high intermediary markups, opaque pricing practices, delayed settlement processes, and centralized custody of user funds — by providing a transparent, smart contract-governed technological infrastructure.

By replacing traditional intermediary functions with programmable smart contracts, BetsWave enables participants to submit service requests, access automated processing settlement, and retain effective custody of their funds until a service transaction is resolved. The Direct Processing model supports peer-to-peer service delivery between consenting participants, while the Standard Processing model provides pooled liquidity for routine service request execution.

The Wave Token (WAVE) serves as the platform's native utility token, facilitating access to platform functions, governance participation, and ecosystem operations. BetsWave does not operate as a centralized service operator, financial institution, investment platform, regulated exchange, custodian, or any form of regulated financial service provider.
