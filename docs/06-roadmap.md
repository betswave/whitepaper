# 6. Development Roadmap

> **Forward-Looking Statement Notice:** All milestones, timelines, and feature descriptions set out in this section are forward-looking statements reflecting current developmental intentions only. They are not commitments, representations, or warranties. Roadmap items are subject to change, delay, or cancellation based on technical, regulatory, resource, market, and operational conditions. BetsWave undertakes no obligation to update roadmap milestones. See Section 10.4 for the complete Forward-Looking Statements disclaimer.

| Phase | Key Activities and Milestones |
|-------|------------------------------|
| **2023 Foundation Phase** | Finalization of product vision, technical feasibility assessment, and market analysis. Initiation of MVP development on Pulsechain and Polygon test environments. |
| **2024 MVP to Market Readiness** | Completion and testnet validation of MVP. Establishment of strategic partnerships within the blockchain community. Completion of whitepaper, business documentation, and initial security audit processes. |
| **2025 Platform and ICO Launch** | Enhancement of platform design and Hedera proof-of-concept validation. Parallel ICO marketing and product development. Public platform launch with Wave Token ICO. Commencement of Virtual Analytics Environment development. |
| **2026 Metaverse Expansion** | Feature upgrades, community scaling, and XR roadmap development. Launch of Virtual Analytics Environment. Integration of cross-chain features and XR/multiplexer planning. |
| **2027+ Multiplatform Ecosystem Growth** | Launch of Android, iOS, and XR applications for immersive platform access. Introduction of loyalty programs, referral systems, and DApp integrations. Ongoing marketing, optimization, and platform innovation activities. |
